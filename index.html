The Case of the Vanishing Hair (and Other Hairy Theories)

A laugh-out-loud mystery for curious kids and the follically-challenged alike!

Something strange is happening above Dad’s brow… his once-glorious hair is vanishing! But where did it go—and why?

Armed with a notebook, a wild imagination, and a flair for scientific investigation, one young detective sets out to solve the mystery of Dad’s disappearing hair.

Is it the work of sneaky birds building nests? A belly-button burglar stealing strands? Could Dad secretly be a great ape—or even aging backwards?

With each theory more hilarious than the last, this playful story explores big questions with heart, humor, and heaps of hair.
